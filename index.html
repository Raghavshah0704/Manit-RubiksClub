Welcome to RUBIK's Club

The official platform for creativity and innovation at MANIT. Join us to explore events, teams, and memorable moments.

Welcome to Rubiks club, a cultural club at MANIT, known for its vibrant and dynamic contributions to campus life. The club serves as a platform for students to explore and showcase thier talent in various cultural domains, including music, dance, drama, literature and fine arts. Rubix aims to foster creativity and cultural awareness among students,providing them with opportunity to express themselves artistically and collaboratively.

The Club organizes variety of event, workshop and competitions,throughout the academic year,ranging from cultural fests and talent hunt to theaterical performances and art exhibitions.Rubix plays a pivotal role in hosting MANIT's flagship cultural fests,drawing participation from both within institute and across country.It also encourages students to embrace and celebrate diversity of Indias cultural heritage while staying open to global influences.

Through its activties Rubiks not only enriches the campus atmosphere but also nurtures leadership,teamwork and organizational skills among its members, making it a cornerstone of the cultural landscape of MANIT.
